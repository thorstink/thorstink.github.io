This is my online portfolio.

A place for highlighting some of my work and ideas, mostly technical and robotics related.

In the past 7 years I have professionally developed and implemented algorithms and applications applied to robotic products. Varying from mobile ground vehicles1, 2, 3, a 7 degree of freedom arm4 and currently quadcopter drones at Mainblades5. Originally I was mostly focussed on state-estimation, e.g. odometry, SLAM and localization. Later that was extended by planning and control. Nowadays I consider myself to be a roboticist of many traits with a lot of practical development experience in the domain of embeddex linux and C++.
