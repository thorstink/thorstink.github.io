Hello,

I am Thomas Horstink

This is my online portfolio. A place for highlighting some of my work and ideas, mostly technical and robotics related. In the past 7 years I have professionally developed and implemented algorithms and applications applied to robotic products. Varying from mobile ground vehicles1, 2, 3, a 7 degree of freedom arm4 and currently quadcopter drones at Mainblades5. Originally I was mostly focussed on state-estimation, e.g. odometry, SLAM and localization. Later that was extended by planning and control. Nowadays I consider myself to be a roboticist of many traits with a lot of practical development experience.

  1. LEA, the lean elderly assistant from Robot Care Systems. Unfortunately development has ceased.
  2. Lely Collector, a cow-manure collection robot.
  3. Lely Juno, a feed-push robot.
  4. Smart Robotic's Smart Item Picker based on a Universal Robotics arm.
  5. A Mainblades inspection drone, based on the DJI M300 with custom payload
